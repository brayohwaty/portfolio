COVID AUXIKIT

Covid Auxikit is a complete Personal protective equipment package that contains:
  • Filtered masks apt for sterilization.
  • Re-usable face shields.
  • Portable UV light sterilizer.
    • This kit is designed to minimize risk and exposure to coronavirus infections for health workers at their place of work. Hospitals are high risk areas for COVID-19 infections, therefore health care practitioners are prone to these infections especially now with the sudden increase in volume of patients. Personal protective equipment helps protect them from potentially infectious patients and other hazardous substances used in healthcare delivery. AuxiKit can also be used in home settings when handling an infectious patient being treated at home. Also in sterilizing personal effects such as clothes, phones e.t.c. click here to view the whole project report or Download the PDF.

    PORTABLE DUAL USB CHARGER

    The consumer electronic devices operation relies on mobile power to remain operational when plugged out of the source of AC outlet. Most of these mobile devices utilize rechargeable batteries for supply of portable power. The aim of this project was to design and fabricate a Dual USB Charger for charging USB-powered devices (devices that are chargeable by USB cable), such as smartwatches, blood pressure monitors, glucometers, heart monitors, brain-sensing headbands, pain relief devices, thermoguns, mobile phones, cameras, among many others. The system was found to play a vital role especially in mobile charging cases during travelling. Two AA 1.5V dry cells connected in series and a single lithium battery were the ultimate source of power. AA dry cells are cheap, possess lots of power, have low internal resistance and are easily available everywhere. The 3V DC voltage produced by the AA dry cells (also rechargeable batteries) and lithium battery was suitably converted to 5V then fed to the device. Therefore, we can consider AA dry cells as a substitute for the conventional AC power from the mains. The capacities of the DC voltage supplied by the AA batteries can be upgraded. The power pack also consists of TP4056 chip for protection of single-cell lithium-ion battery against overcharging and undercharging. This circuit has a unique advantage in that it enables charging of our gadgets more efficiently with an expended compatibility range. Furthermore, the final circuit is relatively small, making it suitable to be used as a portable emergency battery charger. Download the PDF to view the whole project report.

    CHAKULA CONNECT

    Chakula Connect is an innovative digital platform designed to empower farmers by connecting them to agricultural markets while also providing valuable information and resources for optimizing agricultural production. This project aims to address the challenges faced by farmers in accessing markets, obtaining agricultural knowledge, and making informed decisions. This project was conceived in response to the Hult Prize 2021 Challenge In 2021 asking youth around the world to build viable food enterprises that would create jobs, stimulate economies, reimagine supply chains, and improve outcomes for 10,000,000 people by 2030. Download the PDF to view the whole challenge description.

    Key Features
    Marketplace Integration: Chakula Connect provides a user-friendly online marketplace where farmers can list and sell their products directly to buyers, including wholesalers, retailers, and consumers. Buyers can search for specific products, view product details, and place orders seamlessly.
    Geolocation Services: The platform incorporates geolocation technology to help buyers locate nearby farms and farmers, facilitating local and sustainable sourcing. Farmers can also find nearby collection centers, storage facilities, and processing units.
    Crop and Livestock Management Tools: AgriConnect offers a suite of tools and resources for crop and livestock management. Farmers can access weather forecasts, pest and disease alerts, and best practices for crop cultivation and animal husbandry.
    Market Insights: The platform provides real-time market data, including price trends, demand forecasts, and market analysis. Farmers can make informed decisions about what crops to plant and when to sell.
    Educational Content: AgriConnect offers a comprehensive library of articles, videos, and tutorials on various agricultural topics. Farmers can access training materials, guides, and expert advice to enhance their farming knowledge and skills.
    Community and Support: A community forum and chat feature enable farmers to connect, share experiences, and seek advice from peers and experts. Customer support is available to assist users with any platform-related issues.
    Mobile App Integration: AgriConnect is accessible through a mobile app, ensuring that farmers in remote areas with limited internet access can still benefit from the platform.

    Project Goals:
    Market Access: Facilitate direct access to markets for farmers, reducing the role of intermediaries and increasing farmers' profits.
    Knowledge Dissemination: Disseminate essential agricultural knowledge and resources to farmers, enabling them to make informed decisions.
    Improved Farming Practices: Promote sustainable and best farming practices through expert advice and access to weather data and pest/disease information.
    Data-Driven Decision-Making: Empower farmers with real-time market data, enabling them to optimize crop selection and pricing strategies.

    Implementation:
    AgriConnect will require a team of software developers, data scientists, agricultural experts, and customer support personnel. The platform will be developed using scalable and secure cloud technologies. Partnerships with agricultural organizations, government agencies, and local cooperatives will be crucial for reaching farmers and providing up-to-date information.

    Impact:
  • Improved livelihoods for farmers through increased market access and better pricing.
  • Enhanced agricultural productivity and sustainability through access to knowledge and resources.
  • Reduction in post-harvest losses due to better market planning.
  • Strengthening of local agricultural economies by connecting farmers with local markets.

    • Sustainability:
  • Revenue generation through transaction fees and premium content subscriptions.
  • with government agencies and NGOs for funding and outreach.
  • Continuous improvement and feature updates to meet evolving farmer needs.

    • Chakula Connect represents a transformative initiative that harnesses the power of technology to bridge the gap between farmers and markets while equipping them with the knowledge and tools needed to thrive in modern agriculture.

    AUTOMATED CHICKEN FEEDER

    The SMS Notification Automated Poultry Feeder is an automatic feeding and watering device for poultry. The project's main objective is to significantly reduce human labor requirements within the poultry structure. This will create fewer disturbances to the chicken and reduce the causes of bacteria inversion as humans are the main carrier of bacteria to the poultry area. This project develops a prototype to convey the remaining feed weight to the user with SMS notifications, allowing the user to specify the time to feed the poultry. In addition, it develops a prototype to automate the watering system and feedings. The user will handle poultry more quickly and efficiently in this project. It is made up of automated equipment such as Arduino Uno microcontroller, which will be interfaced with sensors such as Load cell, GSM module, Liquid Crystal Display, DHT11 temperature and humidity sensor, Keypad and servo motor. The DHT11 temperature and humidity sensor will measure the temperature and humidity of the structure and fluctuations dealt with manually. Automated SMS poultry feeder is more efficient than the poultry feeding manual technique. The project thereby reduces the time for feeding the poultry and increases the production of the poultry.
